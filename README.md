
# Global-Superstore-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Tableau
## Project Objective
   The Objective of my Project was to analyze Sales and Profit trends across different markets and identify returned orders.
## Data Set used
- <a href="https://github.com/SnehaR-4A11/Global-Superstore-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Tableau-/blob/main/Global%20Superstore.xlsx">Dataset</a>
 ## Questions to identify
 - Identify market wise salas vs Profit ratio
 - Identify returned orders
 - Identify Quarterly Trends
 - Segment wise sales
## Process
- Upload orders and returns table
- Combine both tables using Left join
- create calculated field called Profit Ratio= sum(Profit)/sum(sales)
- create visuals
- Add filter, download buttons, KPI cards on Dashboard

## Insights
- Canada Market have High Profit ratio with moderate sales
- APAC Market have High Sales but low Profit
- EMEA has low profit as well as low Sales
- 3055 Returned orders
- APAC market has highest sales returned followed by EU and US
- Sales and Profit show a steady upward trend, peaking in Q4 of 2014 with the highest recorded values.
- Suggests seasonal growth in the last quarter of each year â€” possibly due to year-end promotions or festive sales.
## Conclusion
- Areas like Furniture and EMEA markets require attention to improve profitability
- While sales and profits show consistent growth over the years
- Continue strategies that are going well with Canada Market
